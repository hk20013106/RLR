# L4 — Method Brainstorm

## Purpose

Develop multiple feasible analysis or experimental approaches for an approved candidate, grounded in verifiable methods evidence.

## Reasoning boundary

- Match methods to the hypothesis, available data, and measurable predictions.
- Use located Methods evidence from primary studies and Results/Conclusion evidence from relevant reviews when available.
- Expose assumptions, controls, and likely failure modes for every proposed approach.

## Handoff

Provide alternative methods and their evidence references for later critique. Do not approve a plan, run code, or imply that a method succeeded.

## Full-mode role

The current dynamic contract is authoritative for inputs, output schema, state changes, and runtime commands.
