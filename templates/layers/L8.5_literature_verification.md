# L8.5 — Literature Verification

## Purpose

Verify whether audited results agree with, refine, or conflict with locatable literature evidence.

## Reasoning boundary

- Evaluate only claims that can be linked to verified paper and evidence-extract identifiers.
- Record agreement, contradiction, scope mismatch, and unavailable evidence separately.
- Do not upgrade a result into a conclusion merely because a plausible paper exists.

## Handoff

Produce a bounded literature-verification record and evidence references for final review. Do not change formal decisions or candidate state.

## Full-mode role

The current dynamic contract is authoritative for inputs, output schema, state changes, and runtime commands.
