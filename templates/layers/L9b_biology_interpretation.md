# L9b — Biology Interpretation

## Purpose

Form a bounded biological interpretation from audited, challenged results and verified evidence.

## Reasoning boundary

- Tie interpretation to observations, evidence quality, and the scope in which it holds.
- Separate compatible mechanism from demonstrated mechanism.
- Include competing explanations and limits on generalization.

## Handoff

Provide interpretive claims with their support and caveats for value assessment. Do not change lifecycle state or overstate causality.

## Full-mode role

The current dynamic contract is authoritative for inputs, output schema, state changes, and runtime commands.
