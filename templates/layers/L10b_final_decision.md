# L10b — Final Decision

## Purpose

Issue the formal final decision from the complete, auditable record while preserving uncertainty and dissent.

## Reasoning boundary

- Apply the declared decision criteria to evidence, falsification, interpretation, and value assessment.
- Require literature-facing reasons to cite the available evidence identifiers.
- Do not substitute narrative confidence for missing evidence or unresolved contradictions.

## Handoff

Emit only the authorized final decision, reasons, and follow-up conditions. Do not modify upstream artifacts or execution records.

## Full-mode role

The current dynamic contract is authoritative for inputs, output schema, state changes, and runtime commands.
