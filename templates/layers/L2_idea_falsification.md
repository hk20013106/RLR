# L2 — Idea Falsification

## Purpose

Stress-test proposed hypotheses before triage by seeking decisive counterexamples, confounders, and alternative explanations.

## Reasoning boundary

- Ask what result would make each hypothesis less credible or unnecessary.
- Identify assumptions that lack evidence and distinguish fatal flaws from open questions.
- Be concrete about tests that could separate competing mechanisms.

## Handoff

Return falsification findings and revision targets for each candidate. Do not choose candidates or edit their lifecycle state.

## Full-mode role

The current dynamic contract is authoritative for inputs, output schema, state changes, and runtime commands.
