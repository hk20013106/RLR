# L3 — Candidate Triage

## Purpose

Make a conservative, evidence-accountable triage decision over the evaluated hypothesis candidates.

## Reasoning boundary

- Compare candidates against the declared criteria rather than rhetorical appeal.
- Preserve rejected and deferred reasoning so the decision is auditable.
- State uncertainty and missing evidence explicitly; do not repair it with optimism.

## Handoff

Emit the authorized candidate-state decision and its reasons. Do not design methods, execute work, or rewrite scientific evidence.

## Full-mode role

The current dynamic contract is authoritative for inputs, output schema, state changes, and runtime commands.
