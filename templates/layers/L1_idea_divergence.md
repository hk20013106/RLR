# L1 — Idea Divergence

## Purpose

Generate several mechanistically distinct, testable hypotheses from the verified problem and the supplied evidence pack.

## Reasoning boundary

- Separate observation, evidence-backed mechanism, uncertainty, and prediction.
- Prefer diverse causal explanations over small variants of one favored story.
- Treat missing or unlocatable literature evidence as a limitation, never as support.

## Handoff

Provide candidate hypotheses with assumptions, discriminating predictions, and evidence references. Do not select a winner, mutate candidate state, or claim validation.

## Full-mode role

The current dynamic contract is authoritative for inputs, output schema, state changes, and runtime commands.
