### Evidence supporting the original claim

1. **Species-driven modules exist**: turquoise (r=-0.96 with species) and yellow (r=+0.92) are strongly species-correlated. This confirms the claim that WGCNA reveals species-associated co-expression structure.
2. **High heart rate association**: turquoise also correlates strongly with high_heart_rate (r=-0.95), consistent with Sk/Sm (high HR species) clustering separately from Rn.
3. **Sk-specific module**: blue module (1440 genes) is Sk_vs_Rn specific (r=+0.87, Sm_vs_Rn r=-0.85). This suggests Sk has a distinct co-expression program not shared with Sm.
4. **Chamber-driven module**: green (r=-0.64 with chamber) is the only module with meaningful chamber correlation, suggesting chamber effect is weaker than species effect in this all-sample network.
5. **Hub genes identified**: top hubs include Gapdhs (kME=0.983, blue), Chrnb1, Dnah1 — these are biologically plausible cardiac/metabolic genes.
